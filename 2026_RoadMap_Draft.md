# InnerSource Commons Africa – 2026 Roadmap (Draft)

> **Status:** Draft  
> We welcome feedback from the community. Please open an issue or use the **feedback** label to share suggestions.

---

## Purpose of This Roadmap

2026 is a year focused on rebuilding and consistency for InnerSource Commons Africa.

After a quieter period, our focus is to:

- Re-establish a steady community rhythm
- Maintain achievable commitments
- Create space for organic growth as capacity allows

This roadmap prioritizes sustainability and long-term community health.

---

## Core Commitments for 2026

The community commits to the following goals:

- **Monthly virtual community calls**
- **Maintenance of community resources** (README, links, recordings)
- **One in-person or hybrid community meetup** (subject to feasibility)
- **A year-end community reflection**

All other activities depend on available volunteer capacity.

---

## Q1 2026 (January – March): Reset & Alignment

### Community Reset
- Reconfirm the purpose and value of InnerSource Commons Africa.
- Align on focus themes such as learning, sharing experiences, and connecting practitioners.

### Infrastructure
- Refresh the GitHub repository:
  - README
  - Roadmap
  - Code of Conduct
  - Contribution guidelines
- Confirm primary communication channels (e.g. Slack, mailing list).

### Community Calls
- Hold monthly virtual community calls.
- Use the February call to introduce the 2026 roadmap and invite participation.
- Calls remain informal and discussion-driven.

---

## Q2 2026 (April – June): Consistency & Engagement

### Monthly Community Calls
- Continue monthly calls with rotating topics or open discussion.
- Invite guest speakers when coordination allows.

### Content Sharing
- Share notes, links, or recordings from calls when available.
- Reuse existing InnerSource Commons materials where appropriate.
- Encourage community members to share experiences and resources.

### Outreach
- Engage with existing African tech communities where opportunities arise.
- Partnerships are explored based on mutual interest and capacity.

---

## Q3 2026 (July – September): Community Engagement & Events

### In-Person or Hybrid Meetup
- Plan an in-person or hybrid community meetup.
- Location and format depend on volunteer availability and resources.
- Focus on community connection, discussion, and knowledge sharing.
- If in-person is not feasible, host an extended virtual session.

### Continued Community Calls
- Maintain the monthly call cadence.
- Highlight community stories and lessons learned.

### Mid-Year Check-In
- Review what is working and what can be improved.
- Adjust plans as needed.

---

## Q4 2026 (October – December): Reflection & Planning

### Community Reflection
- Hold a year-end community call to reflect on:
  - Participation
  - Value created
  - Sustainability of efforts

### Annual Summary
- Publish a written summary covering:
  - Activities completed
  - Key learnings
  - Challenges
  - Considerations for 2027

### Looking Ahead
- Decide whether to:
  - Continue with the current model, or
  - Increase scope and ambition in 2027.

---

## Guiding Principles for 2026

- **Consistency**
- **Clear communication**
- **Volunteer-driven collaboration**
- **Adaptation based on community energy**

---

*This roadmap will evolve based on participation and capacity.*
