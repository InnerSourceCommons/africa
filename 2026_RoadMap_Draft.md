# Roadmap Draft for InnerSource Commons Africa – 2026 Update

This roadmap is a draft, and we welcome your feedback! Please use the **feedback** label to submit your thoughts and suggestions.

---

## Q1 2026 (January – March 2026): Planning, Foundation Strengthening & Community Alignment

**Key Focus:** Refreshing community foundations, aligning on the 2026 vision, and preparing the ecosystem for expanded participation.

### Refine Vision and Mission
- Reconfirm the community’s 2026 goals and key focus areas.
- Re-align on core objectives: collaboration, knowledge-sharing, and InnerSource adoption across Africa.

### Strengthen Infrastructure
- Review and improve communication channels (Slack, GitHub, mailing lists).
- Update the InnerSource Commons Africa GitHub repository structure.
- Refresh README, Code of Conduct, Contributing Guide, and roadmap.

### Branding & Identity
- Update branding assets if needed and ensure consistent use across platforms.
- Grow social media presence (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, YouTube).

### Community Alignment Sessions
- Host online town halls to introduce the 2026 plan.
- Present updates, introduce contributors and leadership, and invite new participation.

---

## Q2 2026 (April – June 2026): Education, Outreach & Skills Development

**Key Focus:** Expanding awareness, deepening learning opportunities, and strengthening regional engagement.

### Educational Content & Webinars
- Run a structured webinar series on InnerSource fundamentals and practical case studies.
- Create new educational guides, videos, and blogs tailored for African audiences.
- Highlight relevant InnerSource Commons materials for onboarding.

### Community Meetings
- Continue regular community meetings for collaboration, updates, and mentorship.
- Invite guest speakers, African tech leaders, and InnerSource experts.

### Expanded Outreach
- Partner with African tech hubs, developer communities, universities, and open-source groups.
- Identify emerging regional leaders and strengthen country-level nodes.

---

## Q3 2026 (July – September 2026): Engagement, Events & Community Growth

**Key Focus:** Activating the community through events, collaboration activities, and a major in-person/hybrid gathering.

### Events (Virtual + In-Person)
- Organize workshops, hackathons, and thematic learning sessions.
- Host the primary in-person gathering or hybrid community meetup in September 2026.

### Project Engagement
- Encourage contributions to ongoing InnerSource practice guides, translations, case studies, tools, and learning resources.
- Celebrate contributors and highlight success stories.

### Mid-Year Assessment
- Review progress against the roadmap.
- Adjust strategies based on community input and participation data.

---

## Q4 2026 (October – December 2026): Review, Metrics & Future Planning

**Key Focus:** Consolidating insights, evaluating community health, and preparing for 2027 roadmap planning.

### Roadmap Review
- Conduct a full review of activities, progress, and community feedback.
- Identify new goals for 2027 based on impact and growth patterns.

### Community Health Metrics
- Evaluate engagement, contributions, events, and collaborations.
- Strengthen reporting standards for community health insights.

### Annual Report
Publish the 2026 InnerSource Commons Africa Annual Report, showcasing:
- Achievements  
- Impact across regions  
- Challenges  
- Strategic goals for 2027

- Updated visual infographic of the roadmap for community distribution coming soon.
